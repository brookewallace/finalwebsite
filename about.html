A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Brooke Wallace and I am a photographer based in Cleveland, Ohio. I have had a passion for photography and film for as long as I can remember. It is a way to document the moments you hope to cherish for a lifetime and the many beautiful places you will come across.

I have experience photographing weddings, portraits, commercial products, and several other subjects. When working with clients, my goal is to always tell a visual story, ensuring that they walk away with memories they can frame forever.
